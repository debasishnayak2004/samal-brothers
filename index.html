who are we

We design kitchens that breathe better

At Samal and Brothers, we have been serving our customers for over 15 years with a strong commitment to quality and affordability. Specializing in chimney products , we take pride in offering durable, stylish, and efficient solutions that enhance modern kitchens.

Our journey began with a simple vision – to make premium kitchen chimneys accessible to every household at the most affordable prices . Today, we are known for our wide range of products, trusted service, and long-lasting customer relationships.
